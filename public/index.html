About SUP Yoga

Stand Up Paddleboard yoga is the art of practicing asanas (poses) on a board or mat floating on a calm body of water. Classes are designed specifically to be practiced on water with modifications and ease for every level of yogi!

About Citrus SUP Yoga

Maggie, the traveling SUP yoga teacher, loves sharing how yoga has affected her life in such a positive way and believes that by quieting the mind you allow for success and happiness to enter.

She combines her passion for travel and yoga to bring fun, energetic, and calming SUP yoga classes around the world!
