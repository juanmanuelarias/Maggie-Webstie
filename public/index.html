About

Citrus SUP Yoga

After leading a crazy, hectic life in Los Angeles and dealing with years of anger and grief Maggie found yoga and meditation to help calm the mind and become present, instead of living in the past with those negative feelings.

In 2015 Maggie, completed yoga teacher training in Ajijic, Mexico and has been teaching around the world at festivals, events, and studios ever since.

Her passion is to express how yoga helped her and wants to share her practice with others. Join Maggie for a light hearted, calming yoga class and leave feeling calm, relaxed, and connected to your body and mind.

What is SUP Yoga?

Stand Up Paddleboard yoga is the art of practicing asanas (poses) on a board or mat floating on a calm body of water. Classes are designed specifically to be practiced on water with modifications and ease for every level of yogi!
